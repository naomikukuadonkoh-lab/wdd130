Hi there! My name is Naomi Kukua Sullivan, but I usually go by Kukua. I am from Ghana. In Ghana, if you are named Kukua, it usually means you were born on a Wednesday—a fun cultural detail I have always loved. I enjoy eating spicy foods and nothing beats my favorite Ghanaian dishes. When I am not eating, I am busy being a mom, working on my assignments, or simply enjoying some quiet time indoors. I am currently a student at BYUPathway Worldwide, taking university classes online and working hard toward completing my first certificate early next year. I am excited for the journey ahead and grateful for the opportunity to keep learning and growing!

Ghana

Ghana is a vibrant and welcoming country on the west coast of Africa, full of life, color, and warmth. From the lively streets of Accra to the historic castles along the Cape Coast, every corner tells a story of tradition and pride. You can wander through bustling markets, relax on golden beaches, or taste delicious local favorites like jollof rice, grilled tilapia, fufu and spicy meat or fish soup, tuo zaafi and more. What truly makes Ghana special, though, is its people — friendly, open-hearted, and always ready to make you feel at home. With music in the air, smiles all around, and a strong sense of community, Ghana is a place that captures your heart and stays with you long after you leave.
